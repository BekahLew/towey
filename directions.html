From Silverdale, via Highway 16
Drive 30 miles, approximately 38 minutes from Silverdale, WA
  1. Follow Highway 16 south. Take the Purdy exit.
  2. Go down the hill, at the second stop light turn right onto highway 302 W toward Purdy/Shelton 
  3. Cross over Purdy Bridge. Follow highway 302 to next business district
  4. As you pass by the 76 gas station ease into the left hand turn lane
  5. Turn left onto Creviston Dr NW 
  6. Follow all the way to Minter Creek Fish Hatchery
  7. After you pass the hatchery take the first left onto  126th Avenue Kp N 
  8. Once you clear the intersection set your odometer to zero.
  9. Go 1.9 miles (do not look at street signs)
  10. At 1.9 miles you will see my road on the left, 128th Ave Ct Kp N
  11. Turn left onto 128th, follow to the end of the cul-de-sac, we are the yellow house with a green roof on your left
